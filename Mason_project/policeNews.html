Four people charged after alleged escape from Hamilton isolation facility


SAT, JUL 25 . SOURCE: 1 NEWS




Four of the five people who allegedly escaped from a Hamilton isolation facility last night have been charged, police said this morning.


Source: 1 NEWS

A 37-year-old year woman and three young people were detained in Hamilton at about 7.50pm yesterday, he said.
